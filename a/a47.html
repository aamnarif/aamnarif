Another feather in our cap!

We have been participating in the ‘Annual Flower Show’ organized by the Horticulture Society of Pakistan for the past four years from 2012 to 2014.This year we participated in the category ‘School Garden’ our teachers and students worked with enthusiasm and created amazing landscapes in our already beautiful  school garden. Their efforts proved fruitful and we have bagged the 2nd prize. Congratulations!
Parents were also invited to experience the phenomenon of spring at the DPS garden. They lauded the efforts of the management, teachers and students.
Commissioner Municipal Corporation DMC (East), Administrator DMC (East), Superintending Engineer DMC (East) and Director Parks visited our school garden and were impressed by its scenic beauty. They initiated their program”Clean and Green – Peaceful Sindh” by planting trees in the school campus. The Commissioner also gifted   trees to the school to be planted by the students.
The CEO, Principal and students also participated in the plantation.
