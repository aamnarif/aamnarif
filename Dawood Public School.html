Dawood Public School added 4 new photos to the album: Teachers Day at DPS.
October 7 at 9:07am 路

Teachers Day is celebrated on 5th Oct to honour teachers for their contribution to the society. Teachers are the backbone of a society. They guide, love, discipline and inspire students to dream and soar high to achieve their goals.
Students of DPS celebrated their teachers in their own special way. To express their appreciation, care and love the little pre-primary students presented flower bouquets, handmade cards and chocolates and sang songs to make their mentors feel sp...ecial. Maira of class IV-E primary section brought delicious homemade cup cakes for her teachers. Safa Mubeen of class III expressed her love for her teachers by offering chocolates.
The secondary students lauded the efforts of their teachers by presenting flowers and Thank You cards for continually inspiring them to strive for goals and also for being their friends and mentors.
